About This App

Where the study material comes from, and how it was built.

The source material

Every question in this app is based on the official Ohio Driver Manual — "Digest of Ohio Motor Vehicle Laws" (HSY 7607), published by the Ohio Department of Public Safety and Ohio BMV. It is the single official study document for the Ohio knowledge test.

Read the official manual (PDF)

The 10 official questions

Ohio publishes an official Sample Knowledge Test of 10 questions — the only real, BMV-authored questions available to the public. All 10 are included here, marked with a Official BMV badge so you can spot them.

How the rest were built

By Ohio law (Admin. Code 4501:1-1-08) the written test has two parts — a minimum of 20 questions on traffic laws and 20 on road signs — and the manual states you need 75% to pass the 40-question test. The actual questions come from a secured pool Ohio does not publish, so no app can show "the real test." Instead we wrote questions matching that structure and style:

  • Read the manual — extracted the text of all 55 pages.
  • Pulled verified facts — each question's answer is a rule stated in the manual (e.g. residential speed 25 mph, fire hydrant 10 ft).
  • Cited the page — every question links to the exact manual page that proves its answer.
  • Matched the official split — 20 traffic-law and 20 road-sign questions, and the app shows your score for each section.

These are labeled manual-verified. Nothing appears in the app without a source you can check.

The road signs

Sign images are the official federal MUTCD standard signs (public domain, U.S. Department of Transportation) — the same signs Ohio uses. Each sign is labeled with its standard code.

Not affiliated with the Ohio BMV. This is a free, community-built study tool. Always confirm current rules against the official manual before your test.
