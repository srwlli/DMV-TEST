## About the Study App

This app is **NOT** affiliated with the Ohio BMV. It is a free, community-built study tool designed to help students prepare for the official knowledge test using material derived from the official Ohio Driver Handbook and verified practice questions.

All questions are based on official BMV test content and Ohio traffic laws as documented in the driver handbook.
