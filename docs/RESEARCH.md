## Executive Summary

The Ohio driver's permit (knowledge) test is a 40-question multiple-choice exam administered by the Ohio Bureau of Motor Vehicles (BMV) covering traffic laws, road signs, and safe driving practices. Students must score 75% (30/40) to pass, with independent section pass requirements.

---

## Official Test Specifications

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Total Questions** | 40 multiple-choice |
| **Road Signs Section** | 20 questions (visual identification) |
| **Traffic Rules Section** | 20 questions (text-based) |
| **Passing Score** | 30 correct (75% minimum) |
| **Section Pass Requirement** | 15/20 on each section independently |
| **Age Requirement** | 15½ years old minimum |
| **Testing Cost** | $23.50 |
| **Test Format** | Multiple choice (4 options per question) |
| **Vision Screening** | Required in addition to knowledge test |

## Core Test Topics

### Road Signs (20 Questions)

#### Categories
1. **Regulatory Signs** (45 signs) — Black & white enforcement signs
   - Stop, Yield, Speed Limit, Turn Restrictions, Lane Control
   - Do Not Enter, Wrong Way, One Way

2. **Warning Signs** (51 signs) — Yellow diamond-shaped hazard alerts
   - Curve, Intersection, Pedestrian, Bicycle, School Crossing
   - Railroad Crossing, Slippery, Steep Grade, Narrow Bridge
   - Divided Highway Ends, Deer/Animal Crossing

3. **Guide Signs** (5 signs) — Directional and informational
   - Route Numbers, Destinations, Exits, Rest Areas

4. **Work Zone Signs** (6 signs) — Construction and maintenance
   - Construction Ahead, Flaggers, Lane Closure, Detour

#### High-Miss Topics
- **Pavement Markings** (32.2% miss rate) — Yellow lines (no passing), white lines (lane separation)
- **Traffic Intersection Rules** (31.5% miss rate) — Right-of-way at simultaneous arrivals
- **Emergency Vehicle Recognition** — Right-of-way procedures

### Traffic Rules & Safe Driving (20 Questions)

#### Speed & Following
- Residential area default: **25 mph** (unless posted)
- Rural freeway default: **70 mph**
- School zones with children present: **20 mph**
- Safe following distance: increases in poor conditions
- Icy/snowy driving: reduce speed + increase following distance

#### Right-of-Way
- Simultaneous arrival at intersection: **car from RIGHT has priority**
- Pedestrians: **always yield**, even if pedestrian is wrong
- School buses (flashing red lights): **STOP from either direction**, remain stopped until lights stop
- Emergency vehicles: pull right, yield right-of-way
- Bicycles: entitled to full lane width

#### Turning & Lane Changes
- Right turn on red: **allowed AFTER complete stop** if no interference with crossing traffic
- Solid yellow line on your side: **NO PASSING**
- Turning: check mirrors, use turn signal, verify lane clear

#### Child & Passenger Safety
- **Before starting engine:** All occupants must be buckled
- **Infant restraints (under 4 / under 40 lbs):** Approved car seat ONLY
- **Child safety (4-8 years):** Booster seat UNLESS 4'9" or taller
- **Rear-seat passengers:** Seat belts mandatory for all ages

#### Alcohol & Drugs (Young Driver Focus)
- **Under 21 BAC limit:** 0.02% (zero-tolerance law)
- **Over 21 BAC limit:** 0.08% (impaired driving threshold)
- **Alcohol effect:** Only **TIME** helps; coffee/fresh air do not reduce impairment
- **Drugs:** Marijuana, prescription drugs, over-the-counter meds can impair

#### Special Situations
- **Studded tires:** Legal November 1 – April 15 ONLY
- **TIPIC holder (learner):** Cannot drive midnight–6am without licensed adult (21+)
- **Failure to stop after crash:** 6-point violation (serious)
- **Parking:** Know No-Parking signs, handicapped spaces, fire hydrant zones

#### Parking & Stopping
- Stop line location (no markings): **point nearest intersecting roadway where you can see approaching traffic**
- Angle parking vs. parallel parking rules
- Parking brake use (on hills, when leaving vehicle)

## Verified Sample Questions

### Road Signs Examples

1. **"This road sign indicates:"**
   - Roundabout ahead
   - No passing zone
   - Left turn yield on green
   - **CORRECT: Traffic signal ahead**

2. **"This sign means:"**
   - Slippery when wet
   - Tow-away zone
   - Steep downgrade
   - **CORRECT: Slippery when wet**

3. **"This sign means:"**
   - Keep right
   - Low shoulder
   - No parking at any time
   - **CORRECT: Keep right**

### Traffic Rules Examples

1. **"What is the most important driving technique to avoid crashes when driving in icy or snowy conditions?"**
   - Add extra weight to vehicle
   - Get off highways quickly
   - Engage 4WD
   - **CORRECT: Reduce speed and increase following distance**

2. **"A flashing red traffic signal at an intersection has the same requirements as which of the following?"**
   - Slow sign
   - Yield sign
   - **CORRECT: Stop sign**
   - Intersection sign

3. **"When you approach a stopped school bus with flashing red lights on a two-lane road, you must:"**
   - Slow down and check for children
   - Proceed cautiously around the bus
   - Only stop if children are visible
   - **CORRECT: Stop and remain stopped until the lights stop flashing**

4. **"Infants and children under what age require an approved car seat?"**
   - Under 2 years
   - **CORRECT: Under 4 years old AND under 40 lbs**
   - Under 6 years
   - Until they reach 60 lbs

5. **"When permitted to turn right on a red traffic signal?"**
   - Only with a green arrow
   - Only if no pedestrians are present
   - **CORRECT: After stopping and checking that the turn won't interfere with crossing traffic**
   - Anytime traffic permits

6. **"A solid yellow line on your side of the center road means:"**
   - Proceed with caution
   - **CORRECT: You may not pass**
   - Two-way traffic ahead
   - Speed limit changes ahead

7. **"For drivers under 21 in Ohio, the blood-alcohol limit is:"**
   - 0.04%
   - **CORRECT: 0.02% (zero-tolerance law)**
   - 0.08%
   - Any detectable amount

8. **"Parent or guardian signature is required when the applicant is under what age?"**
   - 16
   - **CORRECT: 18**
   - 21
   - Not required

9. **"Motorcycles are entitled to:"**
   - The bicycle lane
   - **CORRECT: The same full lane width as other motor vehicles**
   - Half the width of a lane
   - The width of the sidewalk

10. **"Before switching on the ignition, you should:"**
    - Be sure only backseat passengers have buckled
    - Be sure only adults have buckled
    - Be sure only children have buckled
    - **CORRECT: Be sure all vehicle occupants have buckled seat belts**

11. **"When dealing with pedestrians, a driver must:"**
    - Make sure pedestrian is aware of your vehicle
    - **CORRECT: Always yield right-of-way, even if pedestrian is wrong**
    - Yield only when legally entitled
    - Sound horn near crosswalk

12. **"What helps overcome alcohol's influence?"**
    - Tomato juice/lime
    - Hot coffee
    - Fresh air
    - **CORRECT: Time**

13. **"Studded tires are legal:"**
    - October 1 through March 31
    - **CORRECT: November 1 through April 15**
    - December 1 through March 15
    - October 15 through April 30

14. **"TIPIC holder nighttime driving restriction:"**
    - Cannot use highways
    - **CORRECT: Must be accompanied by parent from midnight to 6 a.m.**
    - Cannot carry passengers
    - Must display special permit sticker

15. **"When you see right-of-way at intersection with simultaneous arrival:"**
    - Car from left has priority
    - Car honking first
    - Faster car passes first
    - **CORRECT: Car from right has right-of-way**

## App Learning Methods (Pedagogical Approach)

### Mode 1: Study Mode
- Learn by category (all road signs, then all rules)
- Instant feedback with explanations
- Read explanation → retake question
- No time pressure
- Progress saved per category

### Mode 2: Quiz Mode
- Full 40-question test (20 signs + 20 rules)
- Timed: 40 minutes recommended
- Mimic real test format exactly
- Final score + question-by-question breakdown
- Focus on weak areas after completion

### Mode 3: Weak Areas Drill
- Auto-detect frequently missed questions
- Repeat questions until 3/3 correct
- Prioritize high-miss topics (pavement, intersections)
- Track improvement over sessions

### Mode 4: Road Signs Gallery
- Visual reference — tap any sign to learn
- Sign shape, color, meaning, rules
- "When you see this..." explanations
- Search by name or category
- Offline-cached

### Spaced Repetition Engine
- Question appears again after: 1 day, 3 days, 1 week, 2 weeks
- "Mastered" label after 2 perfect scores
- Weak questions surfaced more frequently
- Session history preserved
